# Sample JD: Frontend Developer — Outsource Agency

**Company:** DevBridge Agency
**Location:** Ho Chi Minh City, Viet Nam (hybrid)

## About the role

DevBridge delivers web and mobile projects for clients across US, EU, and Australia. We need a skilled Frontend Developer who can jump between projects, adapt to different tech stacks, and deliver quality work under tight timelines.

## Responsibilities

- Develop client-facing websites and web applications
- Work with project managers to understand and clarify client requirements
- Deliver functional UI within project deadlines (typically 1–4 week sprints)
- Handle client feedback rounds and iterate quickly
- Maintain and extend existing codebases across multiple projects simultaneously
- Participate in code reviews and internal knowledge sharing

## Requirements

- 2+ years of frontend development experience
- Strong HTML, CSS, JavaScript fundamentals
- Experience with at least one major framework (ReactJS, VueJS, or Angular)
- Comfortable reading and extending unfamiliar codebases
- Good communication skills for client interaction
- Ability to manage multiple tasks and switch context between projects
- Experience with responsive design and cross-browser compatibility

## Nice to have

- Experience with CMS platforms or template engines (LiquidJS, Handlebars)
- Web accessibility knowledge (WCAG)
- Figma for design handoff
