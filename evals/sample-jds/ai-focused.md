# Sample JD: Frontend Engineer — AI Product

**Company:** Cognify (AI productivity tools)
**Location:** Remote

## About the role

We're building AI-native tools that help knowledge workers move faster. We're looking for a Frontend Engineer who is genuinely excited about working with LLMs, understands agentic workflows, and can ship polished AI-powered interfaces.

## What you'll do

- Build frontend interfaces for AI-powered features (chat, agent UIs, structured outputs)
- Integrate with LLM APIs and streaming responses
- Work closely with the AI/ML team to translate model outputs into usable UX
- Experiment with AI-assisted development workflows to ship faster
- Help define how we build and test AI features on the frontend

## What we're looking for

- 3+ years of frontend experience (ReactJS or NextJS preferred)
- Genuine interest in AI and hands-on experience with LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- Experience building chat interfaces or agent-style UIs is a strong plus
- Understanding of prompt engineering and context management
- Experience with streaming APIs and real-time UI updates
- Comfort working in fast-moving, ambiguous environments

## Bonus

- Experience with agentic workflows (multi-step, tool-calling agents)
- Familiarity with RAG patterns or vector search
- Open-source contributions related to AI tooling
