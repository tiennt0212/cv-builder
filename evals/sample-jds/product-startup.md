# Sample JD: Frontend Engineer — Product Startup

**Company:** Finlo (Series A fintech startup, 40 people)
**Location:** Remote (SEA timezone)

## About the role

We're looking for a Frontend Engineer to join our core product team and help build the next generation of personal finance tooling for Southeast Asia. You'll work closely with design and backend to ship features end-to-end — from scoping to production.

## What you'll do

- Own frontend features from design handoff to deployment
- Build and maintain a ReactJS + TypeScript codebase shared across web and mobile web
- Collaborate with the backend team on API design and data contracts
- Improve performance and reliability of our core dashboard (used by 200k+ users)
- Help define frontend architecture decisions as we scale

## What we're looking for

- 3+ years of frontend experience with ReactJS and TypeScript
- Experience working in a fast-moving product team with high ownership
- Strong understanding of state management (Redux, Zustand, or similar)
- Comfortable with REST APIs and asynchronous data flows (SWR, React Query)
- Experience with performance optimization — you know what causes re-renders and how to fix them
- Bonus: NextJS, experience with financial data or charting libraries

## Why join us

Small team, big ownership. You'll see your work impact real users within days of shipping, not months.
